Feeding America

Feeding America is a non-profit organization that aims to reduce hunger and food insecurity in the United States. It is the largest hunger-relief organization in the country. Feeding America's mission is to feed America's hungry through a nationwide network of member food banks and engage the country in the fight to end hunger. The organization provides food and groceries to people in need through its food banks, pantries, and meal programs, as well as offering nutrition education and support services to families and individuals. Feeding America also works to address the root causes of hunger and food insecurity by advocating for government policies and programs that support those in need.

Midwest Food Bank

Midwest Food Bank is a non-profit organization that works to alleviate hunger and food insecurity in communities across the Midwest region of the United States. The organization collects and distributes food donations to a network of partnering charities and non-profits, including food pantries, soup kitchens, shelters, and other organizations serving those in need. Midwest Food Bank operates several locations in multiple states and provides food and groceries to those in need through partnerships with local organizations. Midwest Food Bank also offers volunteer opportunities to help raise awareness about hunger and food insecurity. The organization's mission is to alleviate hunger and provide hope to people in need by efficiently gathering and distributing food and related products to those who need it most.

No Kid Hungry

No Kid Hungry is a national campaign aimed at ending childhood hunger in the United States. The campaign is run by Share Our Strength, a non-profit organization, and works to ensure that all children have access to nutritious food. No Kid Hungry provides meals to children in need through school breakfast and summer meal programs, connecting families with existing government nutrition programs, and educating families and communities about the importance of nutritious food. The organization also works to improve child nutrition policies and programs at the state and federal levels. No Kid Hungry's goal is to end childhood hunger in America by ensuring all children have access to nutritious food to lead healthy, active lives.

American Nutrition Association

The American Nutrition Association (ANA) is a professional organization for registered dietitians, nutritionists, and other professionals working in the field of nutrition. The ANA's mission is to promote optimal health and nutrition for all people through education, advocacy, and professional development for its members. The organization provides resources and support for its members, including continuing education opportunities, networking events, and advocacy efforts to advance the field of nutrition. The ANA also offers resources and information for consumers on healthy eating, nutrition, and lifestyle, with a focus on the latest science-based recommendations and guidelines.

MyPlate

Created by the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), MyPlate serves as a visual representation of all food groups recommended for a healthier diet. MyPlate is divided into five sections, each representing a food group: fruits; vegetables, grains, proteins, and dairy.
